Smart Meter

What?

  • A GSM-based electronic device to collect data on environmental and agricultural parameters such as soil moisture, humidity, temperature, and current and voltages of solar power systems.

    • How?

  • Exploited Node MCU(ESP32) as the processor, GSM/GPRS as the communication medium, and MQTT protocol for sending data to the server.
  • Designed circuit board prototype and used CadSoft EAGLE to create schematic, and 2 layer PCB boards.

    • Result

  • The reliability of the collected data were observed to be more than 90%.
